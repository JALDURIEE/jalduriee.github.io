Privacy Policy

Thank you for using our Frame Genie app (hereinafter referred to as "the app"). We highly value your privacy and personal information protection. Please read the following privacy policy carefully to understand how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information Collection

The app collects your information only in the following cases:

  • Access to Photos: When you choose to read photos from the album for editing, the app needs access to your album.
  • Save Photos: When you save the edited photos to the album, the app needs write permission.

2. Information Use

The information we collect is used only for the following purposes:

  • Read Photos: Allow you to select photos from the album for editing.
  • Save Photos: Save your edited photos to the album.

3. Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with any third parties. All information is processed only on your device and is not uploaded to our servers or any external services.

4. Information Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. These measures include but are not limited to:

  • Data Encryption: Encrypting data during transmission.
  • Access Control: Restricting access to information to only those who need it.

5. User Rights

You have the right to revoke the app's access to the album at any time. You can do this through your device's settings:

  • Revoke Read Permission: Go to "Settings" > "Privacy" > "Photos", select the app and set it to "Deny".
  • Revoke Write Permission: Go to "Settings" > "Privacy" > "Photos", select the app and set it to "Deny".

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We recommend that you review this privacy policy regularly to stay informed about our information protection measures.
