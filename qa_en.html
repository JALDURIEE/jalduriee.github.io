1. Why can't I read the photo parameters?

  • The photo itself must display the shooting parameters in the album, otherwise the parameters are lost.
  • Most third-party image editing software will lose the shooting parameters after editing the image. Professional software like Lightroom and Photoshop will not lose the parameters when exporting images.
  • Images transferred via social software like WeChat will also lose the shooting parameters.

2. Slow photo saving time

Possible reasons:

  • Due to the need to synthesize a blurred background and the original image for the blurred background template, if the photo resolution is too high, the saving time will be relatively slow. It is recommended to lower the image quality in the settings.

3. Tips for use

  • Border selection: Try different styles of borders and use various tools in the toolbar to edit the image to find the best effect for the photo.
  • Filter tools: The filters built into the app are carefully selected, so it is recommended to try different filter styles.
  • Blur templates: The degree of background blur in the blur templates can be customized, and the position of the image and shooting parameters can be freely moved.
